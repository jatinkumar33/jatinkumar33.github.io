Cupsgalore (Australia) — SEO & Digital Marketing

Client: cupsgalore.com.au • Industry: custom paper cups & packaging manufacturing • Market: Australia • Platform: Shopify • Duration: ~1.5 years

Challenge

  • Website had limited organic visibility and weak SEO setup
  • Product pages needed better metadata and keyword alignment
  • Indexing + sitemap health needed monitoring via GSC
  • Social content needed consistent creative execution

Actions implemented

  • Technical SEO: sitemap submission/monitoring, coverage checks
  • On-page SEO: titles, meta descriptions, headings, URLs
  • Content: keyword-aligned product descriptions
  • Social: posts + creatives + reels editing for campaigns

Results snapshot (Google Search Console — 3 months)

Impressions39.1K
Clicks235
Avg Position14.6
CTR0.6%

Indexing snapshot

  • Indexed pages: 84
  • Not indexed: 30 (tracked and monitored)
